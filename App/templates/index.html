Our Services – Varmala Events

Turning Traditions into Grand Celebrations

Royal Destination Weddings

Experience the charm of royal weddings at iconic destinations like Udaipur, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Pushkar, and more. We manage everything from venue selection, theme creation to hospitality – making your wedding a palace-like affair.

Resort & Banquet Bookings

Tie the knot at the finest resorts and banquet halls in Jaipur. Whether it's a heritage fort, luxury resort, or traditional haveli-style venue – we arrange the perfect setting as per your budget and theme.

Themed Décor & Stage Design

From Rajwada-style varmala stages to floral mandaps and mehndi setups, we specialize in creating stunning decor inspired by your vision — with traditional Rajasthani touches like bandhej, gota patti, peacocks, elephants, and mandalas.

Cultural Entertainment & Folk Artists

Bring your event to life with live folk performances, including Ghoomar dance, Kalbeliya, Shehnai, Dhol, and puppet shows. Add the cultural heartbeat of Rajasthan to your special day.

Photography & Wedding Films

Capture every royal moment with our professional wedding photographers and cinematographers. From pre-wedding shoots to varmala reels – we create timeless memories.

Complete Wedding Management

From invitations, logistics, guest handling, to honeymoon planning – we manage it all. You enjoy the rituals, we handle the rest with royal hospitality and professional finesse.
